Data Drieven Recommender Service For Wealth Management

By leveraging power of machine learning, financial institutions can provide better peronsalized customer experience, and enhance efficiency of selling strategies.

  • 01 Realize customer-centric outcomes

    Deliver superior and personalised experience, and improve cross-sell and upsell opportunities with 360-degree views of customer engagement.

  • 02 Power optimaization and data-driven decisions

    Use granular insights to target potential customer groups and effective selling strategies. Optimize marketing spend, maximize selling potential.

  • 03 Faster and timely insights

    Different from traditional data warehouse running in batch mode, AIRS predicts recommendation immedidately based on latest portfolio and real time market data. Thanks to data streaming architecture in Apexa.

 

Services

AIRS provide various recommender services, aim to help your business do less gain more.

Product Recommendation

Collect the most favorite investment products, tailor-made portfolio proposal for each customer.

Target Customer Recommendation

Find out the most suitable customers for every newly launch product - save time, save cost.

Cross Selling Recommendation

Analyze all existing customers, never miss any cross selling opportunities.

Any Recommendation

By leveraging neural network and deep learning, different kinds of recommender services can be acrhieved.
